Forecasting Wizards

Created Tuesday 27 February 2018

Forecasting wizards is a game for cryptocurrency traders on 4chan's business and finance board. I made the game to show anons volatile coins and allow them to make educated guesses with a feedback mechanism that rewards correctness.

Round 3

I have been working on round 3 development. I wanted to change up the coin guessing mechanism and have overlaid a numbert chart from 100 to 0. Players now have to guess a number between 0 to 100 based on where they think the current price position is.

Round 2

Since there was a warm reception to round 1 I added some more development.
- Country flags
- User avatars
- Technical charts
- Winner leaderboard
