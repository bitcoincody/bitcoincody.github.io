Two strats I will share with you. Don't mistake simplicity for lack of utility.

First, understand that compound interest can turn seemingly trivial gains into massive ones with consistency and a bull market. Bears will be harder with day trading.

Okay, two strategies. Both depend on high volatility. I trade crypto because there are no laws for day trading minimums and because volatility is high. I trade almost exclusively ETH but will swing trade others.

Strategy 1: Level 2s. Look at the order books. Learn how the topography of the buy/sell orders can carve out a potential path for brief spikes (I'm talking .5 - 1.5%; if you think those gains are dumb, compound a 1% gain x amount of times.) Learn to spot fake sell/buy walls from manipulators. In a bull market you can learn without stop lossing but this is a huge risk. Don't put in more than you're willing to lose.

Strategy 2: Learn the rhythms of the market. Watch ceilings and floors. If something hits a floor for a second time, and sentiment hasn't changed, ride that sucker up. I literally bought ETH yesterday a $911 because that was the second time it hit that $910 floor in the last day or two. I then sold at $950. That was for literally zero effort and a day of waiting. You do the math.

With all of these, never market buy. Set limit orders and commit unless new information arises
